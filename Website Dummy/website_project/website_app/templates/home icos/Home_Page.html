ICoSITeR 2021

International Conference on Science, Infrastructure Technology and Regional Development (ICoSITeR) is an annual
international conference 2020 which is held by the Institut Teknologi Sumatera (ITERA). This year, ICoSITeR 2020 presents a theme: “Industry 4.0-based Science and Technology for National Independence and Advancement”. ICoSITeR 2020 is expected to connect
between companies, organizations, stakeholders, and academia who are active in the fields of technology and infrastructure. ICoSITeR 2020 is expected also to be a place for knowledge exchanges, breakthroughs, and ideas related to the development of various fields in an effort to increase the nation’s competitiveness on a global level. ICoSITeR 2020 aims to gather professors, researchers and scholars all over the world. Sponsors will receive recognition participation either before, during or after the event through various publications and material. They may also enjoy significant interactions with speakers and participants during the event. Institut Teknologi Sumatera will hold ICoSITeR 2020 on 23-25 October 2020. All honorable authors are kindly encouraged to contribute to and help shape the conference through submissions of their research abstracts, papers, and posters. Papers submitted to ICoSITeR 2020 will be reviewed by technical committees of the conference. Selected papers will be published in IOP Conference Proceeding (Scopus Indexed).
